About Me

I am a Master's student in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at Korea University, studying under the guidance of Prof. Sangpil Kim in the Computer Vision Lab. My research primarily focuses on advancing generative AI and deep learning-based computer vision, with a strong emphasis on bridging the gap between 2D visual synthesis and 3D spatial understanding.
Currently, I am working on creating highly realistic and temporally coherent digital content for next-generation immersive platforms, exploring topics like 3D-aware structural reconstruction and diffusion-based generation frameworks.

Research Interests

  • Core Areas: 3D Reconstruction, Generative Model, Multi-modal Learning, Computer Vision, Deep Learning
  • Applications: AI Safety, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
