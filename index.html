            <h2 id="about-me">About Me</h2>

            <p>

                I am a Master's student in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at <a href="https://www.korea.edu/">Korea University</a>, studying under the guidance of Prof. Sangpil Kim in the <a href="https://kuaicv.com/">Computer Vision Lab</a>. My research primarily focuses on advancing generative AI and deep learning-based computer vision, with a strong emphasis on bridging the gap between 2D visual synthesis and 3D spatial understanding. 

                <br />

                Currently, I am working on creating highly realistic and temporally coherent digital content for next-generation immersive platforms, exploring topics like 3D-aware structural reconstruction and diffusion-based generation frameworks.

            </p>


            <h2 id="research-interests">Research Interests</h2>

            </ul>
